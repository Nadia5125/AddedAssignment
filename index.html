About me


Love the challenge to learn new things to improve myself and to upgrade to the new trends and help solve problems.

I am detail orientated and a good team player. I’ve done volunteer PR work to assist a women’s group in Japan at the Tokyo American Club. I’ve done volunteer work on website content of a non-profit organization and organized the raising of funds for basic needs such as medical, food and clothing supplies. I worked with USAID to provide more than 500 shelters to refugees. I like to travel to different countries to learn new thing and understand other cultures. I enjoy learning about how other countries do things and conduct business. It is good to understand what opportunities outsiders can bring to develop relationships with other nations to help create jobs and help improve lives. As of today, technology connects people around the world from their fingertips. Business conferences can be done via video webinar and education can be attended online, and much more. This helps those who are less fortunate to have opportunities to make the impossible, possible. It gives them a chance to learn and get the education to stay connected with the outside world. All of this thanks to TECHNOLOGY! I want to be part of creating this type of technology that connects the world that brings changes to people’s lives and makes a difference.
